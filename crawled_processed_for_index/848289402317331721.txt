     <h1 id="ROS_Installation_Options">ROS Installation Options</h1> 
     <span class="anchor" id="line-2"></span>
     <span class="anchor" id="line-3"></span>
     <p class="line862">There is more than one ROS distribution supported at a time. Some are older releases with long term support, making them more stable, while others are newer with shorter support life times, but with binaries for more recent platforms and more recent versions of the ROS packages that make them up. See the <a href="/Distributions">Distributions</a> page for more details. We recommend one of the two versions bellow: <span class="anchor" id="line-4"></span><span class="anchor" id="line-5"></span></p>
