     <h2><a href="/criticalpath">The Critical Path</a></h2> 
     <p>The Critical Path is a talk show contemplating the causality of success and failure in the evolving story of mobile computing and related industries. Using Apple as a lens to look at existing and emerging tech markets, we try to understand what it means to be great.</p> 
     <p>Hosted by <a href="http://twitter.com/anders94">Anders Brownworth</a> and <a href="http://twitter.com/asymco">Horace Dediu</a>.</p>
